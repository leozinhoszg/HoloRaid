Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Policy describes how HoloRaid (“we”, “application”, “service”) collects, uses and shares information when you use the website, application and Discord bot. By using HoloRaid, you agree to the practices described here.

HoloRaid is a fan-made tool for organizing Operations (raids) in Star Wars: The Old Republic, integrated with Discord. It is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by BioWare, Electronic Arts, Lucasfilm or Disney.

1. Information we collect

  • Discord account (via OAuth2): your Discord ID, username, nickname, avatar and, when available, email. You authorize this access when signing in with Discord.
  • Game characters: name, faction, class/combat style, discipline, role (Tank/Healer/DPS), item level and your PvE progression (defeated bosses and Tier score) — as provided by you.
  • Raids: raids you create or sign up for, your role and status (confirmed/waitlist).
  • Notifications: if you enable push notifications in the app, we store the device token (Android/Web) to deliver them.
  • Authentication: session tokens (JWT and refresh) to keep you signed in.
  • Bot configuration: when a Discord server installs the bot, we store the server ID and the announcement channel ID, plus references to published raid messages.
  • Administrative logs: administration actions (e.g. promoting a user) are logged for auditing.

2. How we use the information

  • Authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • Display your characters, your PvE progression and your Tier.
  • Organize raids, build the roster, validate eligibility (faction/Tier) and manage the waitlist.
  • Publish and update raid announcements in the configured Discord channels and send notifications (push in the app and/or the bot's direct message).
  • Show aggregated community statistics (e.g. most played operations, most active players).

3. Sharing and visibility

We do not sell your information. Some information is visible to other users by the nature of the service:

  • Your username, avatar and the data of your signed-up characters appear in the roster of raids and in Discord announcements.
  • Your username and avatar may appear in aggregated rankings (community dashboard).

We share data with providers strictly to operate the service:

  • Discord — for login (OAuth2) and for the bot's operation (commands, embeds, direct messages). Use of Discord follows Discord's own privacy policy.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — only if push notifications through the application are enabled, to deliver messages to your device.
  • Hosting and database provider — to store and serve the service's data.

We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Retention and deletion

We keep your information for as long as your account exists. You can request deletion of your account and associated data (characters, sign-ups and tokens) via the contact below; when you delete the account, the linked data is removed.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information (for example, short-lived rotating session tokens and secrets kept out of the code). No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children

HoloRaid is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by Discord in your region). We do not knowingly collect data from children below that age.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top indicates the current version. Continued use of the service after changes means acceptance of the updated version.

8. Contact

Questions about this Policy or requests regarding your data: contato@holoraid.fun.
